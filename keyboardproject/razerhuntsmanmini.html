Razer Huntsamn Mini

The Razer Huntsman Mini is an exceptional gaming keyboard with a compact form factor. Its small size is great for minimalist setups and to allow for more space to move the mouse. The build quality is excellent, it has per-key RGB backlighting, and every key is macro-programmable.
